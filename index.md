# Jason Dunn
**PhD Candidate | Department of Economics, Boston University**

![Profile Picture](assets/img/prof_pic.jpg)

Hello, and welcome to my website! I am a PhD candidate at the Boston University Department of Economics. My research specializes mainly in economic history, labor, migration, and education. I formerly worked as a research associate at the [St. Louis Federal Reserve](https://www.stlouisfed.org/research). 

My research with Fernando Leibovici on international shipping and trade has been featured on the [Wall Street Journal](https://www.wsj.com/articles/new-studies-find-unprecedented-impact-from-supply-chain-turmoil-11641587220), [Barron’s](https://www.barrons.com/articles/baltimore-key-bridge-collapse-us-economy-3f39bccc), and [The Economist](https://www.economist.com/business/2024/12/03/how-painful-will-trumps-tariffs-be-for-american-businesses).
