### About Me
Hello, and welcome to my website! I am a PhD candidate at the Boston University Department of Economics. My research specializes mainly in economic history, labor, migration, and education. I formerly worked as a research associate at the St. Louis Federal Reserve. My research with Fernando Leibovici on international shipping and trade has been featured on the Wall Street Journal, Barron’s, and The Economist.
